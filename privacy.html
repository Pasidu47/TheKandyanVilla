Your Privacy Matters to Us

At The Kandyan Villa, we are committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you visit our website or stay with us. We will never sell your data to third parties.

1. Overview

This Privacy Policy applies to The Kandyan Villa ("we", "our", "us"), located at 532/30, Heerassagala Road, Heerassagala, Kandy, Sri Lanka. It covers the personal data we collect when you:

  • Visit and browse our website
  • Make a reservation or booking enquiry
  • Stay at the property as a guest
  • Contact us via email, phone, or our contact form

2. Data We Collect

We only collect information necessary to provide you with our services. This includes:

Personal Details

Full name, nationality, date of birth, ID/passport number

Contact Information

Email address, phone number, home address

Payment Information

Transaction data (processed via secure third-party gateways — we do not store card details)

Booking Details

Arrival/departure dates, room type, special requests

Technical Data

IP address, browser type, device info, pages visited

Feedback & Reviews

Guest reviews and satisfaction survey responses

3. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used strictly for the following purposes:

  • Reservation management – to process and confirm your booking
  • Guest experience – to personalise your stay and fulfil special requests
  • Communications – to send booking confirmations, pre-arrival information, and receipts
  • Payment processing – to securely handle transactions
  • Legal compliance – to meet hotel registration and reporting obligations under Sri Lankan law
  • Service improvement – to analyse website usage and improve our offerings (only in aggregate, anonymised form)

4. Sharing of Information

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share limited data only in the following circumstances:

  • Payment Processors: Trusted third-party payment gateways (e.g., PayPal) to handle transactions securely.
  • Legal Obligations: Authorities when required by Sri Lankan law, such as police registration of foreign guests.
  • Service Partners: Trusted third-party service providers (e.g., email services) bound by confidentiality agreements.

5. Cookies

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience. Cookies are small files stored on your device that help us understand how visitors use our site.

Essential Cookies

Required for the website to function properly

Analytics Cookies

Help us understand site usage patterns

Preference Cookies

Remember your settings and preferences

You can set your browser to refuse cookies, but this may affect your website experience. By continuing to use our website, you consent to our use of cookies.

6. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your personal data against unauthorised access, loss, destruction, or alteration. These include:

  • SSL encryption for data transmission on our website
  • Secure, access-controlled storage of guest records
  • Regular staff training on data protection best practices
  • Payment card data handled exclusively by PCI-compliant third-party processors

7. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

Right to Access

Request a copy of the data we hold about you

Right to Correction

Ask us to correct inaccurate data

Right to Deletion

Request erasure of your data where applicable

Right to Object

Object to processing of your data for marketing

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at kandyanvilla111@gmail.com. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes for which it was collected, and to comply with legal obligations. Specifically:

  • Guest registration records are kept for a minimum of 5 years as required by Sri Lankan hotel regulations.
  • Financial records are retained for 7 years for accounting purposes.
  • Website usage data (cookies/analytics) is typically retained for no more than 26 months.
  • Marketing communications data is kept until you unsubscribe or request deletion.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Your continued use of our website after changes are posted constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
