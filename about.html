Heritage

Embracing the Spirit of Kandy

The Kandyan Villa was born out of a desire to share the timeless beauty of Kandy and true Sri Lankan hospitality with travelers from around the world. Nestled in the serene setting of Heerassagala, our property provides an escape into nature.

Our philosophy centers around providing personalized service while minimizing our footprint on the stunning natural environment that surrounds us. Every detail, from the locally sourced ingredients in our restaurant to the cultural aesthetics of our rooms, reflects the rich mosaic of Sri Rican heritage.

  • Authentic Sri Lankan architecture and design elements
  • Eco-friendly practices and sustainable local sourcing
  • Warm, personalized service from our dedicated local team
